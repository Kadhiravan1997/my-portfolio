Background

Where Data Meets People

I'm Kadhir — an analytical and HR professional who has spent 13 years working at the intersection of people strategy and data intelligence. My career has been defined by hybrid roles that rarely exist neatly in an org chart but are increasingly essential to how modern enterprises operate.

At Royal Dutch Shell, Tiger Analytics, and Siemens, I have led initiatives where understanding human behaviour and interpreting data were not separate functions — they were the same conversation. The result: decisions grounded in evidence, and teams that actually trust them.

I thrive in ambiguity, enjoy translating complex datasets into plain language, and believe the best HR strategy is one built on what the numbers are actually saying — not what we assume they mean.

Analytics Mindset

Workforce analytics, HR metrics dashboards, predictive modelling for attrition, hiring funnel analysis, and data-driven talent planning.

Human Resources

Talent acquisition, performance management, compensation strategy, employee experience, and HR business partnering across global teams.

The Hybrid Edge

Operating across both worlds means I can ask the right questions of data and of people — and know which answer to trust in each situation.
